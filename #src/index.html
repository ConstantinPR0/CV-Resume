About me

Self-development and moving forward desire pushed to study of frontend development, so I became very happy when I finally realized, that programming is my vocation, the dream of my whole life lies in. Little histiory (spoiler - click here to open)

I graduated from Kurgan State University with a degree in “Technical and Environmental Safety”. This specialty is not related with information technologies at all, but all the time I was studying, I tried to automate my study with help of computer and different programs, for example, formulas in Excel helped me to write my course works quickly. Then I went to the army. In the army I had a goal to get to the rank of a junior sergeant and my work in the army was directly related to the programs on computers. I graduated from the army with the rank of junior sergeant. Next, when I came home, I took a job as a safety engineer. This time I also had the goal of becoming an executive in my field, and a year later I took a management position with an auditing company that provided services to other companies.

Another six months later, I was hired by a fairly large energy company in the city, took an executive position in my field, and supervised occupational safety specialists in eight branches in different cities. My salary was above average in Russia, but at that point, after several months of work, I realized that I don’t want to advance in this field of work anymore. As it did not develop me in any way, I began to feel that I was standing still, and suddenly it occurred to me to leave my job and find myself in something that would bring pleasure, where I could learn new things and improve myself every day.

I started with web design and did a short 7-day marathon on creating a landing page, which I finished, and learned how to use Photoshop. Since then I periodically draw something in Photoshop for different projects. Next, I decided to get acquainted with the layout (programming still seemed complicated for me, so I decided to start with something simple) - and I did a 3-day marathon to create my own mini-page, written in HTML and CSS, after writing the code in VS-code, I realized that this is what I am interested in. After a while, my friend invited me to the HTML-Academy where I started to learn JavaScript, and I liked it. I solved all the additional tasks in JS and passed all the subjects in a month, and after that I was finally convinced that programming is what I want to devote my life to. Sometimes I write code at night and I don't feel sleepy at all, but I’m trying to watch over my health. Then I discovered learn.javascript.ru and accidentally found the RSSchool course in the comments. I have now completed JS/FE PRE-SCHOOL Stage#0 and have enrolled in JS/Frontend-development Stage#1. Now I can say with full confidence - I will become a programmer - it's only a matter of time.
